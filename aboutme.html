About Me

    Hi, my name is Sergei Diakov, I am a geologist and an artist.
    I had a long professional career of conducting exploration for mineral deposits of copper and gold around the world working for several leading mining companies.
    With PhD in economic geology, I led exploration teams to several successful world-class discoveries in Asia and South America. In pursuing my professional goals, I accumulated significant experience in different cultural environments around the globe.
    While traveling to remote corners of the world, I enjoyed various landscapes and unforgettable natural scenes. My eyes capture a combination of amazingly impressive natural beauty and geological structure created by Mother Earth.
    I started developing artistic skills from early childhood in the Ukraine. During my education at the university in Moscow, I had a great opportunity to spend time with a famous Russian artist Oleg Grosse in his studio. Profound philosophical thinking of beauty and love conquering the world has occupied my thinking since that time. One can find them in my paintings.
    I live in Escondido, California, with my lovely family; my dear wife Alevtina and my son Dmitri. The local nature is becoming a dominating theme in my modern paintings. My art has a thought provoking charge and will make a living treasure for every member of the entire family household to enjoy and inspire.
